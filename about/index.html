About Me

My Story

As a native Italian speaker with a passion for teaching, I've dedicated my career to helping students master the beautiful Italian language. With years of experience in both traditional and online teaching, I've developed a unique approach that combines cultural immersion with practical language skills.

My goal is to make learning Italian an enjoyable and rewarding experience, helping students not only speak the language but also understand and appreciate Italian culture.

Teaching Philosophy

Personalized Learning

Every student is unique, and I tailor my teaching methods to match individual learning styles and goals. Whether you're preparing for a trip to Italy, advancing your career, or simply exploring a new language, I'll create a customized learning plan for you.

Cultural Immersion

Language learning goes beyond grammar and vocabulary. I incorporate Italian culture, traditions, and real-world context into every lesson, making the learning experience more engaging and meaningful.

Practical Communication

My focus is on practical communication skills that you can use in real-life situations. From day one, you'll be speaking Italian and building confidence in your language abilities.
